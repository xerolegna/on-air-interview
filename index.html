A realistic mock interview spoken aloud, adaptive to your answers, and scored by a blunt coach. Speak or type; get feedback on pacing, filler words and structure.

Why a key? This app runs entirely in your browser — there's no server. The interviewer's questions and your feedback are generated by Claude (Anthropic's AI), and calling it requires your personal API key.

How to get one:

  1. Create an account at platform.claude.com
  2. Add a small amount of credit (a full 10-question interview costs a few cents)
  3. Go to API Keys → Create Key, copy the key (it starts with sk-ant-…)
  4. Paste it into the field above

Privacy: your key is sent only to api.anthropic.com, never anywhere else. It's saved on this device only if you tick "Remember key" otherwise it's forgotten when you close the page. Treat it like a password: anyone who has it can spend your credit.

Voice input isn't supported in this browser — you can type your answers.
